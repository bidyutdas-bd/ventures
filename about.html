Our Story

Our Story started in 2013, when we started as a small IT company in a small town, with ambitions to change the way technology is delivered in Schools & Businesses. Since then, we have developed into a multi-faceted organisation with multiple ventures, with the aim of empowering and developing young people as future leaders.

Our Values & Plans For The Future

We are very ambitious and want to make an impact on the community that has given so much to us.

Leaders

There is a lot of talent out there that require the right environment, training and support to become future leaders. With many success stories within our organisation, we aspire to be the go-to place for young people to fulfil their ambitions.

Values

Skills can be taught but values are the ones you bring. We are looking for passionate, hard working and enthusiastic individuals to join us on our journey, and create your own story.

Message From The Founder

Coming from an ethnic background has its challenges. Having worked through multiple roles I know the challenges that can be faced. One thing I have felt always shone was true work ethic in an individual, regardless of their background.

Our team come from various backgrounds, and they have a single thing that makes it work - hard work. With a determination to be the best at their role, our staff are our case studies.

We are where we are because of our staff, and this is something I am very proud of. We would love for you to join us on our journey, whether it is as a customer, a supplier or just a well-wisher, together we are better.
